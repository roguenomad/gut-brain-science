Involve

To engage a global audience and foster collaborations with researchers (Physicians and Scientists) in the field, particularly research emphasis on disorders of Gut-Brain interactions (DGBIs, Irritable Bowel Syndrome and Functional Dyspepsia), gut motility disorders (idiopathic and diabetic gastroparesis, slow transit constipation) and related metabolic disease (obesity and diabetes).

Educate

To increase awareness about gut-metabolic health and disease, promote knowledge about Gut-Brain Science what is going on at front bench to bedside and build a community of Gut-Brain Science lovers through this platform.

Advance

To share the cutting-edge research and developments in the field of gut-metabolic health and disease, focusing research based on the physiological and pathophysiological mechanisms at cellular and molecular resolution connecting Gut-Brain-Microbiota nexus in DGBIs, gut motility disorders, diabetes, and obesity.
